Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before downloading.

Yes — TreadLeft is completely free to download and use. There are no subscriptions, no paywalls, and no hidden fees. Core features including shoe tracking, mileage alerts, and deal finding are all included at no cost.
Strava is the primary way to sync your runs automatically — connect once and every run updates your shoe mileage. You can also log runs manually inside the app if you don't use Strava.
Mileage is pulled directly from Strava's GPS data, so it's as accurate as your device's GPS. We display distances to one decimal place and match them to whichever shoe you had assigned in Strava at the time of the run.
TreadLeft tracks each shoe against its manufacturer's recommended mileage limit (typically 500–800 km). You'll receive a push notification as your shoe approaches and reaches its limit — before the foam breaks down and causes injury.
When your shoe nears retirement, TreadLeft automatically searches 19 retail partners across multiple currencies to find the best current price for your exact model. Deals are refreshed nightly so you always see up-to-date prices.
