Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the TreadLeft mobile application (the "Service"). It explains how TreadLeft ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects information.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

  • Account information: When you sign in, we associate your account with identifiers from your authentication provider (e.g., Strava).
  • Preferences: Unit preferences (km/mi), theme settings, and configuration.

B. Information We Receive from Strava

If you connect Strava, we access information necessary to provide the Service:

  • Profile information (athlete ID, name, image).
  • Activity data (runs, distance, dates) for mileage calculation.
  • Gear data (shoes/gear list) for wear tracking.

C. Diagnostics / Telemetry

We collect limited diagnostics (device model, OS version, error logs) to maintain reliability. We do not use this for advertising.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to provide the Service (sync data, track mileage), maintain reliability, provide support, and improve security.

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers operating the Service, for legal reasons, or in business transfers.

4. Data Storage and Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information, though no transmission method is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain information as long as necessary to provide the Service. You may request deletion.

6. Your Choices

Disconnecting Strava: You can remove TreadLeft access in Strava settings.

Access/Deletion: Contact us to access, correct, or delete your data.

7. Contact

Email: product@treadleft.app
